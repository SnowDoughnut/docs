## Measurement review

**Five minutes, once a quarter. Confirming everything still holds is a normal, useful outcome.**

- Is the north star still right?
- Are the tier 1 KPIs still right?
- Did any definition change this quarter?
- Is the attribution model still appropriate?
- Did we validate attribution this quarter?
- Are sources of truth still correct?
- Any open questions in the [data dictionary](/marketing-reporting-data-dictionary) older than two quarters?

Changes recommended → record in the data dictionary's decisions and changes section, with an effective date.

<Accordion title="Concept review: Measurement review">
  This is the section every team wants to skip, and the one that keeps the whole setup from drifting quietly out of date. Most quarters, the honest answer to every question is "yes, still holds," which is itself worth recording, since it's the evidence that the dictionary hasn't gone stale.
</Accordion>
