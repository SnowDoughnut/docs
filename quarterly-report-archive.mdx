title: "Quarterly report archive"
description: "A running log of every quarterly review, with its headline result and the decisions it produced, so a past quarter stays findable without opening a dozen decks."
---

One row per quarterly review as it goes out. Include the decisions made, not just the result: a quarterly review exists to produce decisions, and the log is more useful when it shows what those were.

| Period | Link | Headline result | Decisions made |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| _E.g. Q2 2026_ | _E.g. \[link\]_ | _E.g. Contribution margin \$312k, 4% ahead of plan_ | _E.g. Shift 15% of Q3 budget from Google Search to Meta_ |
| \[add quarter\] |  |  |  |

**Naming pattern:** `{account}_qbr_{YYYY-Q#}`

**Retention:** Indefinitely.
