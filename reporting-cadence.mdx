title: "Reporting cadence"
description: "How often each report runs: weekly, monthly, quarterly, and yearly."
---

How often each recurring report runs, and what each cadence is for: a weekly report catches what needs attention now, a monthly report checks the target, a quarterly review checks the strategy, and an annual review sets next year's plan. See [weekly](/weekly-reporting-process), [monthly](/monthly-reporting-process), [quarterly](/quarterly-reporting-process), and [yearly](/yearly-reporting-process) reporting processes for the steps behind each cadence, and [report writing concepts](/report-writing-concepts#cadence-changes-what-a-report-is-for-not-just-its-length) for how to match reporting frequency to decision frequency.

Every cadence above weekly is a rollup of the cadence below it, not a new report; see [data quality and rollup concepts](/data-quality-and-rollup-concepts) for how that works.
