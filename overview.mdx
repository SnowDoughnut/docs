This knowledge base holds the shared conventions, standards, briefs, and processes the marketing team works from. Set these up once, in order, and every campaign, naming, and reporting decision after that has something to check against.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read through the concepts">
    Start with the [naming conventions overview](/naming-conventions-overview) and the [glossary](/glossary) to get the shared vocabulary.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your brand guidelines">
    Fill in [brand voice and tone](/brand-voice-and-tone) and the rest of the [brand system](/brand-system-overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your naming conventions">
    Work through [campaign](/campaign-naming-conventions), [ad set / ad group / variant](/ad-set-group-naming-conventions), [ad](/ad-name-conventions), [customer profile](/customer-profile-naming-conventions), [file](/file-naming-conventions), and [event](/event-naming-conventions) naming conventions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your experiment ID conventions">
    Define your team's pattern in the [experiment ID workbook](/experiment-id).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first experiment brief">
    Use the [experiment brief](/experiment-brief) template for your next test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your UTM conventions">
    Fill in [UTM standards](/utm-standards) once you've read [UTM parameters](/utm-parameters).
  </Step>
</Steps>
