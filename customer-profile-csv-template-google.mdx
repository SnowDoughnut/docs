---
title: "Customer profile CSV template (Google)"
description: "Downloadable CSV template for uploading customer profile segments to Google Ads."
---

Use this template to format customer profile segments for upload to Google Ads customer match audiences. Fill in one row per profile member identifier, following the [customer profile naming conventions](/customer-profile-naming-conventions) for the segment name.
