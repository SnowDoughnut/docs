description: "How this knowledge base is organized, and where to go depending on what you're trying to do."

This knowledge base is organized by tab, and most pages fall into one of a few types:

- **Concepts** explain the thinking behind a system (naming, experimentation, tracking) before you fill anything in.
- **Shared standards & briefs** are the fill-in workbooks and dictionaries your team builds once and reuses: naming conventions, UTM standards, creative standards, customer profiles.
- **Platforms & channels** hold platform-specific tutorials, SOPs, and how-tos.
- **Processes** walk through a recurring workflow end to end, like launching a campaign or reviewing an experiment.
- **Templates** and **Reports & Library** hold reusable starting points and the record of finished work.

If you're new, start at the [overview](/overview) for the setup order, or jump straight to [quick starts](/quick-starts) for the fastest path into a specific task.
