---
title: "Customer profile CSV template (Meta)"
description: "Downloadable CSV template for uploading customer profile segments to Meta Ads."
---

Use this template to format customer profile segments for upload to Meta Ads custom audiences. Fill in one row per profile member identifier, following the [customer profile naming conventions](/customer-profile-naming-conventions) for the segment name.
