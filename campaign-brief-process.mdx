Campaigns are at the heart of digital marketing

A marketing campaign is a sets of strategic activities that promote a business's goal or objective

They span multiple channels, mediums, creatives, people ...

Marketing campaigns can be big or small

Marketing campaigns may have multiple stakeholders, teams, and deliverables to coordinate.

launching a successful campaign can feel like a herculean task.

Marketing campaign management involves  key steps to make your initiative a success, including: setting the goals identifing your target audiences and ICPs defining budget planning timline selecting marketing channels creating content launching content measuring success
