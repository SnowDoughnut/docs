The list of [KPIs](/glossary#kpi) the team tracks, each defined once so everyone reports it the same way. Referenced from the [marketing campaign brief](/marketing-campaign-brief) KPI section and [dashboard standards](/dashboard-standards).

KPI definitions, formulas, and targets are fully specified in the [marketing reporting data dictionary](/marketing-reporting-data-dictionary), including the [north star metric and KPI tiers](/marketing-reporting-data-dictionary#kpi-tiers) and a [metric formula block](/marketing-reporting-data-dictionary#metric-formulas) per KPI. Start from the [ecommerce](/ecommerce-metrics-starter-pack) or [sales-led](/sales-led-metrics-starter-pack) starter pack rather than a blank page.
